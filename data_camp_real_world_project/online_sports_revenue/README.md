Online Sports Revenue Analysis

In this project, I merged the brands, finance, info, and reviews dataframes, removed rows with missing values, and categorized products by price label (Budget, Average, Expensive, and Elite) to compare how different price categories perform across brands. Additionally, I analyzed whether description length influences ratings and the number of reviews left by users.

Outputs
- Adidas vs. Nike Revenue by Price Category
  Produced a grouped summary showing product counts and mean revenue across price categories.

- Description Length vs. Reviews
  Generated a grouped summary displaying average ratings and total reviews by description length bins.
