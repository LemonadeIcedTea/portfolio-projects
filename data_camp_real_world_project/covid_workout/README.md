Covid Pandemic Workout Analysis

In this project I am tasked with identifying which year workout piqued a lot of people's interest, what was the most popular workout during the peak covid pandemic and currently between home workout, gym workout and home gym workout. I'm also tasked with finding which country had the highest interest for workout among 3 countries and which country had the highest interest in home workout between Malaysia and Philippines.

Otuput
- Year of Peak Workout Interest
  Imported the workout dataset and plotted monthly worldwide workout searches to identify the year when interest peaked.

- Most Popular Workouts During the Pandemic vs. Now
  Used the three_keywords dataset to plot trends for home workout, gym workout, and home gym searches, comparing popularity during the pandemic and in the present.

- Country with the Highest Workout Interest
  Analyzed the workout_geo dataset to compare workout interest across the United States, Australia, and Japan.

- Malaysia vs. Philippines – Home Workout Interest
  Examined the three_keywords_geo dataset to compare home workout interest between Malaysia and the Philippines.
