Electric Vehicle Charging Trend

In this project I'm tasked with identifying how many electric vehicles were sold in 2018, plotting the trends for public charging ports, private charging ports and total sales and lastly identify whether the total sales, number of public and private ports showed the same trend during 2015-2018.

Output
- Total Electric Vehicle Sales in 2018
  Imported the relevant dataframes, grouped sales by year, and summed totals. Printed the yearly sales series and extracted the 2018 value.

- Sales, Public, and Private Charging Port Trends
  Merged the private and public charging port dataframes with the total sales dataframe, then plotted all three trends in a single figure for comparison.

- Do Electric Vehicle Sales and Charging Ports Show the Same Trend?
  Yes. From 2015 to 2018, electric vehicle sales and the number of public and private charging ports followed the same upward trend.
