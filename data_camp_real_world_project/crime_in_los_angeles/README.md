Analyzing Crime in Los Angeles

In this project I'm tasked finding which hour of the day have the highest frequency of crimes, which area in Los Angeles had the largest frequency of night crimes, and identifying the number of crimes committed against different victim age group.

Output
- Highest Frequency of Crime
  Imported the crimes dataset, created a column for the hour of occurrence, identified the hour with the highest crime frequency, and visualized crime counts by hour.

- Peak Night Crime Location
  Filtered the dataset to include only crimes occurring between 10 PM and 4 AM, determined the area with the highest frequency of night crimes, and plotted the distribution of night crimes by area.

- Victim Age Group
  Defined bins and labels to categorize victims into age groups, generated a summary table of crimes by age group, and created a visualization to highlight the distribution across categories.
