Investigating Netflix Movies

In this project I was tasked with finding the most frequent movie duration in the 1990s and count how many short actions movies there are in the same decade.

Output
- Most Frequent Movie Duration in the 1990s
  Imported the Netflix dataset, filtered it to include only movies released between 1990 and 1999, and visualized the distribution of runtimes to identify the most common movie duration during that decade.

- Short Action Movies in the 1990s
  Filtered the dataset further to include only action films from the 1990s, then counted how many had a runtime of less than 90 minutes.
