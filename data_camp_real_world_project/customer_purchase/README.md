Analyzing Customer Purchase of Products

In this project, I'm tasked with analyzing 2 types of customers'(returning and new) and their purchase rates during November and December.

Outputs 
- New vs. Returning Customer Purchase Rate
  Generated a list showing which new and returning customers made purchases and which did not during their online sessions.

- Time Spent Among Page Types
  Produced a correlation table comparing Administrative Duration, Informational Duration, and Product-Related Duration.

- Sales Probability Analysis
  Calculated the probabilities of customer purchases resulting in fewer than 100 sales versus at least 100 sales, and visualized the results in a graph.
